Chapter 01 · Excerpt

The Broke Student Trap

It is 11:47 PM on a Wednesday. Rohan is sitting on the floor of his college hostel room in Lucknow, back against the bed, phone in hand. His roommate is asleep. His mother called at 7 PM. It was not a long call — she did not want to say on the phone what she needed to say: that the money his father transferred last week is almost gone, and could Rohan please try to manage with ₹800 until the end of the month?

He is in his third year of a B.Com degree. He is not a bad student. He pays attention in class. He submits assignments. But tonight, he is doing the maths that no professor has ever taught him. He has ₹800. He needs to eat for nineteen days.

He is twenty years old. He is not lazy. He is not stupid. He is simply stuck in a system that taught him everything except how to earn.

Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need any special skills or prior experience to start?
No. Chapter 2 helps you identify skills you already use daily — like writing, organizing data, or using social media — and shows you exactly how to package them into services people will actually pay for.
I'm worried this will affect my studies. How much time does it take?
The system is designed specifically for busy students. It requires just 2 to 3 focused hours per week. The book also includes a framework to protect your grades and manage your time without burning out.
Is this book relevant if I'm a student in Nepal?
Absolutely. While the core frameworks apply globally, the book includes specific benchmark rates in NPR, local payment guides (eSewa, Khalti, Payoneer), and real case studies of Nepali students earning online.
How is this different from other "make money online" guides?
This isn't about dropshipping, crypto, or get-rich-quick schemes. It's a practical, step-by-step manual for building a real, service-based freelance income using zero capital, a smartphone, and the skills you already have.
What if I try the steps and still don't get clients?
The book includes a 30-day commitment framework and exact scripts for outreach. If you do the exercises, send the messages, and still get zero replies, the book shows you exactly how to diagnose the problem and fix your approach.
Can I read a sample before buying?
Yes. Scroll up to the "Free Sample" section to download the full Chapter 1 PDF and see if the writing style and framework are the right fit for you.
